The Story

Mudbone is a consummate musician, singer, songwriter, and producer whose debut double album Rivers & Roads broke into the Top 100 on the Americana Charts independently — and did it twice, for both volumes. The work blends blues, soul, country, folk, and rock and roll into a sound that takes listeners on a journey from the roots of blues and bluegrass to the expansive highways of roots rock.

His time in honky tonks and juke joints honed his guitar skills and deepened his appreciation for the commonalities music creates across every kind of audience. The gospel and bluegrass harmonies of his childhood laid a foundation, while training in the vibrant blues scene of the Mississippi Delta made him not just an artist but a custodian of the Delta's legacy.

He is passionately committed to a single message: Music is the commonality that bridges cultures and fosters understanding. Essentially, Music is Medicine. Whether performing for thousands at large festivals or in intimate bar and club settings, his powerful performances resonate deeply — and they pack a room.
